A hackathon is an event, often held over a fixed duration

Where individuals or teams come together to collaboratively work on solving problems, developing innovative ideas, and building projects. It is typically centered around technology and programming, although non-technical aspects such as design and entrepreneurship may also be involved

During a hackathon, participants work intensively, often overnight, to create a functioning software application, website, hardware prototype, or any other creative project within the given timeframe. They are encouraged to think outside the box, experiment with new technologies, and collaborate with team members or other participants.
